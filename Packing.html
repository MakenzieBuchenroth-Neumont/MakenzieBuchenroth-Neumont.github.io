Packing

A charming pixel-art puzzle game that challenges players to master the art of spatial organization in a race against time.

üéÆ About the Game

Packing is a 2D puzzle game created during a 48-hour game jam. Players take on the role of an employee tasked with efficiently packing various items into a van. With a time limit and limited space, players must strategically arrange boxes to maximize the van's capacity while maintaining stability. The game combines spatial reasoning with time management in a visually appealing pixel-art style.

This project demonstrates the implementation of physics-based puzzle mechanics in a casual gaming experience.
