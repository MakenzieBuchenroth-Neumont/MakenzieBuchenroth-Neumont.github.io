Obsession

A stealth game where obsession takes a dark turn, challenging players to navigate moral boundaries in pursuit of twisted love.

üéÆ About the Game

Created as a 10-week capstone project, Obsession is a 3D stealth game where players assume control of a teenage boy whose unhealthy infatuation drives him to extreme measures. As players navigate through a suburban setting, they must use stealth and strategy to eliminate anyone who gets close to their crush, all while maintaining their facade of normalcy.

This project explores dark themes of obsession and moral decay, wrapped in a tense, atmospheric gameplay experience that challenges players to question their own boundaries.
