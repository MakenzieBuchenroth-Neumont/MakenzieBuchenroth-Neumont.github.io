About me

As a passionate Game and Software Developer at Neumont College of Computer Science, I've immersed myself in both indie and enterprise development, creating engaging experiences across multiple platforms. My journey began with mastering Java fundamentals, but quickly expanded into a diverse technical toolkit including C++, C#, and Unity development.

What sets me apart is my hands-on experience in both solo and team environments. I've independently developed games, including my capstone project Obsession, while also contributing to collaborative projects like DreamWare for Dreamscapes. My technical versatility is demonstrated through projects ranging from 3D stealth games to 2D puzzle experiences, each showcasing different aspects of game development from level design to AI implementation.

Beyond coding, I bring strong design sensibilities to my work, with expertise in Adobe Creative Suite and a keen eye for user experience. This combination of technical and creative skills allows me to create polished, engaging experiences that resonate with players. I'm set to graduate in September 2025, bringing with me ~10 weeks of indie development experience and ~30 weeks of enterprise development through various projects.
