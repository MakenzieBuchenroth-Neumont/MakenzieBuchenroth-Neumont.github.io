Court of Shadows

Created in collaboration with Kolten Morales for the 72-hour Neumont Summer 2023 Game Jam under the theme "You are the villain".

A narrative-driven visual novel that puts players in the role of a royal advisor, where every decision shapes the delicate balance of power in a court of intrigue.

üéÆ About the Game

Court of Shadows is an anime-styled visual novel created during a 72-hour game jam. Players take on the role of a royal advisor in a medieval court, where they must navigate complex political relationships and make crucial decisions that affect various factions' satisfaction. The game combines visual novel storytelling with strategic resource management, creating an engaging experience that explores themes of power, loyalty, and consequence.

This project showcases the implementation of branching narratives in a visual novel format.
